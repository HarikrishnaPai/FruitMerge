Fruit Merge 2048

Merge fruits to reach the Golden Fruit!

Score 0

Goal

Slide fruits across the board and merge matching pairs to grow bigger fruits. Reach 👑 Golden Fruit to win!

Controls

  • Desktop: Arrow keys (↑ ↓ ← →) or Click & Drag
  • Mobile: Swipe up, down, left, or right

Merging

When two identical fruits touch, they combine into the next fruit:

  • 🫐 + 🫐 → 🍓
  • 🍓 + 🍓 → 🍊
  • 🍊 + 🍊 → 🍎

Keep merging to climb all the way to 👑 Golden Fruit (2048).

Scoring & Game Over

  • Each merge adds the new fruit’s value to your score.
  • After every move, a new 🫐 or 🍓 appears in a random empty cell.
  • The game ends when the board is full and no merges are possible.
